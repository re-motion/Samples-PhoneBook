[RM-3484] Separate logical from persistence mapping

Component/s: Data

Resolution: Fixed
Status: Closed


The current ClassDefinition.EntityName and PropertyDefinition.StorageProperty properties should be replaced by a complete persistence mapping model which captures the (storage provider-specific) details of how a ClassDefinition is mapped to underlying persistence concepts, and how a PropertyDefinition is stored by the data source.
